Vijay

Chandrasekaran Joseph Vijay, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is an Indian politician and former Tamil cinema superstar serving as the 9th Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu since May 10, 2026. Founder of Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, he ended decades of DMK–AIADMK dominance after a blockbuster film career with hits like Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, and Leo. Transitioning from his fan-led welfare movement, he now leads a coalition government focused on social justice and welfare reforms.
